Question A) Which principle Hans and Grete are 
using to find they way back, Stack or queue?

Answer A) The stack principle, they are leaving crumbs and finding their way back
by picking up the last one they dropped.

Question B) Which principle does the computer use 
to find its way back then i get “lost” in method calls? 

Answer B) The stack principle, when you call a method it is put on the stack
and if that method calls other methods and so on, those other methods will also be
put on the stack. Once the "last" method is called it will be removed from the stack and
code will contiue from their, repeating that procces until it has returned all the way to main
